MeowgAw is not only a pet-adoption site, but a non-profit organization dedicated to the well-being of stray animals at UP Visayas. Our mission is to contribute positively to the lives of abandoned, abused, injured, and neglected animals in the locality. However, this is only possible with people like you, who share our love for animals.
